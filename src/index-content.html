Welcome!

I am a post-doctoral researcher in the Gribble Lab and the Sensorimotor Superlab at the Brain and Mind Institute in London, Ontario, Canada. I study how the brain creates and controls movement in humans using behavioural tools, biomechanical models, and deep learning.

The aims of this website are to

  • Make my research more easily navigable by bringing together the resources scattered across (too many) platforms.
  • Go beyond single-article narratives to link together observations and findings across studies.
  • Make available resources that may be useful to others.
  • Share pedagogic materials that turned out instrumental for self-learning.
  • Learn to code in HTML and CSS.

-- Olivier
